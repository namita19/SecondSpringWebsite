Perimenopause isn’t an ending — it’s a time of profound transformation. Your hormones shift, energy changes, and what worked before may no longer feel aligned. In this post, we’ll explore evidence-based ways to stay active, energized, and connected to your body’s rhythm.

Understanding the Shift

As estrogen levels fluctuate, your body’s response to exercise evolves. High-intensity workouts may drain rather than uplift you. Ayurveda and Traditional Chinese Medicine teach us to balance this shift — through mindful movement, breath, and nourishment.

What Works

Gentle strength training, walking, yoga, and restorative routines keep your metabolism and mood balanced. Remember — it’s not about pushing harder, but aligning deeper.
