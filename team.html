Meet Our Co-Founders

Namita Mankad

Co-Founder & CEO

Product Strategist, Program Leader & Phoenix Coach

With 15+ years leading global programs and building SaaS platforms, Namita blends discipline and intuition to guide teams through high-stakes initiatives. She’s modernized systems, scaled multi-million-dollar programs, and led teams of 100+ across industries from healthtech to FinTech.

Dr. Neelam Kharod Sell

Co-Founder & Chief Medical Officer

Developmental Behavioral Pediatrician & Holistic Health Expert

As the longtime owner of The Milestones Center, Dr. Sell specialized in evidence-based, compassionate care for children with developmental and behavioral needs. After a fulfilling career, she embraced retirement as renewal — and discovered a new purpose in co-building Second Spring.

She brings the perspective of a physician who bridges modern medicine with nutrition, mental health, and holistic practices, addressing a critical need for perimenopause support with accredited resources and personalized tools.
