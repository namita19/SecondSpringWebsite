Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Namita Mankad LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Second Spring Health website and mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our Service.

Second Spring Health is designed for women navigating perimenopause and menopause. Our goal is to provide personalized wellness insights that combine ancient wisdom with modern science—while keeping your privacy fully protected.

We are not a covered entity under HIPAA but voluntarily align our systems and processes with HIPAA, GDPR, and CCPA/CPRA standards to ensure the highest levels of security, transparency, and respect for your personal data.

This policy applies to all users of our Service, including visitors to our website and users of our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Date of birth and age range
  • Location information (city, state, country)
  • Account credentials and profile information

2.2 Health and Wellness Information

  • Menstrual cycle and symptom tracking data
  • Health and lifestyle details (diet, exercise, sleep, mood patterns)
  • Wellness preferences, goals, and feedback

2.3 Usage Information

  • Device information (type, OS, unique identifiers)
  • Log data (IP address, browser type, session duration, pages visited)
  • Cookies and similar technologies
  • In-app activity and feature interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Deliver personalized wellness insights and symptom recommendations
  • Provide educational content and lifestyle guidance
  • Improve app features and usability
  • Maintain and secure the Service

3.2 Communication

  • Send service updates, reminders, and feature announcements
  • Respond to inquiries and customer support requests
  • Share educational newsletters (with your consent)

3.3 Research and Development

Analyze anonymized data to improve our AI models and wellness recommendations. Conduct aggregated trend analysis to enhance the understanding of women’s health. Develop and test new features that support the perimenopause journey.

All analytics and research are conducted using aggregated or anonymized data, ensuring no individual can be identified from the data used.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data based on the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: When you choose to track health data, receive newsletters, or participate in research.
  • Contract: To deliver the Service you register for and maintain your account.
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve user experience, secure the system, and prevent misuse.

Users may withdraw consent at any time in their account settings.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do Not Sell or Trade Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade personal data for marketing or advertising purposes.

5.2 Limited and Controlled Sharing

We only share data when necessary:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners under strict Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) who assist in hosting, analytics, or support.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: If we merge, acquire, or restructure, your data will remain protected under the same terms.
  • User Consent: When you explicitly authorize specific sharing (e.g., integrations with health practitioners).

All service providers act as data processors and handle your information only under our direct instructions.

6. Data Security

We use industry-leading security protocols to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256 via Neon Database).
  • Access Control: Strict user authentication and role-based access for internal systems.
  • HIPAA-Compliant Audit Logging: Every data access or modification is logged and retained for six years.
  • Session Security: Cookies configured with Secure, HttpOnly, and SameSite=Lax flags.
  • Regular Audits: Continuous monitoring, penetration testing, and employee security training.

Your data is stored securely on U.S.-based servers with GDPR-compliant safeguards for international transfers.

7. Consent Management and User Control

All user consents are recorded in a versioned, immutable audit trail within our system. You can view or revoke your consent anytime through account settings.

“User consent is securely stored with version control and immutable audit trails. Users may review and withdraw consent at any time through their account settings.”

8. Data Retention and Deletion

8.1 Retention Periods

  • Health and wellness logs: Deleted upon account deletion
  • Account data: Removed within 30 days after deletion
  • Transactional/payment data: Retained up to 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Inactive accounts: After two years of inactivity, we issue warnings before anonymization

8.2 Right to Erasure

When you delete your account, data enters a 30-day grace period (soft delete). After this period, all personally identifiable information (PII) is anonymized, while de-identified health data may be retained for research purposes. This ensures your privacy while supporting women’s health research in an ethical, privacy-preserving way.

9. Data Export (Right to Portability)

You can download your data—including profile, preferences, symptoms, and consent history—through our secure self-service portal in JSON format.

10. Automated Decision-Making and AI

Our app uses algorithms to generate wellness recommendations based on anonymized trends and your data inputs. These recommendations do not have any legal or health consequences and are for educational purposes only.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. For users in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission to ensure data protection equivalency.

12. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to personalize experience and track usage trends.

  • Essential cookies support site functionality.
  • Analytical cookies help us improve performance.

You may control cookies through your browser or consent banner. All session cookies are configured securely (Secure, HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax).

13. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your jurisdiction, typically 13–16). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

14. California Residents (CPRA Notice)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion or correction of your data
  • Opt out of data sharing
  • Receive equal service regardless of exercising your rights

You can exercise your rights by contacting us or submitting a “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” request at namita@namitamankad.com.

15. Research and Ethical Use of Data

Second Spring Health conducts only ethical, aggregated research. All research data is anonymized, and no personally identifiable information is shared with third parties or external researchers.

We may publish de-identified trends (e.g., “percentage of women reporting improved sleep”) to advance understanding of women’s health, but individual users are never identifiable.

16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced via email or in-app notification, with the updated date shown at the top. Continued use of our Service after updates constitutes acceptance.
